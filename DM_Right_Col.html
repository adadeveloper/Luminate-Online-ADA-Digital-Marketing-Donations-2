Call us at 1-800-342-2383. If we receive your change prior to the month’s processing we will make the change effective that month. If not, it will take effect the following month.

 

What if I need to pause giving, can I stop at any time?

Yes, while we always get disappointed when this happens, we understand life happens. Just give us a call at 1-800-342-2383. Your first monthly charge will happen on the day you first sign up. After that, your credit will be charged every month on the anniversary day of your initial donation.

Fight Diabetes AND Its Complications

As if living with diabetes wasn’t hard enough, it also dramatically increases your risk of complications. Diabetes doubles your risk of heart attack or stroke. It can cause kidney failure, blindness, and lower limb amputation, the list goes on.

The good news is that research, education and outreach efforts to decrease complications are working! There's been a dramatic decrease in diabetes-related heart attacks they've dropped nearly 58% over the past 25 years and kidney disease has decreased by nearly a third.

Why Camp Matters

A Chance to be a Kid

At camp, kids get to be kids. For many kids it’s the first time they aren’t the kid with diabetes. They can talk openly and freely about their struggles and everyone understands. They build friendships that last a lifetime.

 

Empowerment to Manage Diabetes

Camp teaches kids valuable lessons on how to manage diabetes. They check their sugar levels before and after activities, count carbohydrates at each meal and snack, and many are learn how to figure out the correct insulin dose and give themselves their first insulin injection or move their insulin pump site for the first time.

 

Build Confidence & Independence

For many kids, camp is the first time they have been away from their parents who help them manage their diabetes. Being away from home and tackling intimidating diabetes management challenges, campers gain self-confidence. They make new friends and try new sports and activities - all help build campers’ confidence and become more independent.
